My story

From patient struggle to scientific discovery - the story behind our mission

First Light of Faith

The "optimal" treatment wasn't working for me.

The brain fog and constant fatigue made me a stranger to myself. I couldn't think clearly or quickly, which meant I couldn't work or study.

Life stopped at what should have been my best moment.

Desperate and without strength, I couldn't stop searching for a solution, even if it was at the speed of a lame turtle. Using my academic background, I began reading hundreds of research articles to understand what was happening to me and why the treatment wasn't working.

I finally understood that my symptoms had already been studied by many endocrinologists. They had proposals to solve it: rigorous studies showed that cortisol pumps could mimic the body's natural rhythms and significantly improve quality of life.

Research with hydrocortisone pumps

The Discovery

The Search

Inspired by the research, I searched for commercial pump prices... $3,000. $4,500. $6,500 dollars. My heart sank. Not only were they extremely expensive, but they weren't available in my country.

The Discovery

But I never give up. I expanded my search and found an article about an $89 insulin pump that worked as well as the expensive ones. The researchers had shared everything for free: designs, code, instructions - no patents or barriers.

The Inspiration

At that moment I saw it clearly: we could adapt those designs to create a hydrocortisone pump. We could build hope from science.

Renewed Hope

This gave me hope. I hope that the pump will restore my natural cortisol rhythms - and those of many others! -, eliminate brain fog and fatigue, and allow me to reconnect with the vibrant person I love being.

I want to make a difference again and earn a living for my family.

We created this website with my partner, who has supported me throughout this process, to share this hope and find collaborators.
